Open Hardware Air Pollution sensor

housing

KRADEX Z56JPH housing is selected. Drawings and 3D models are available at manufacturers site.

3D model

Models from KiCad 4.0.7 release are used where available. Other models are in \lib\packages3d\ directory. You must copy them to KiCad KISYS3DMOD directory (i.e. C:\Program Files\KiCad\share\kicad\modules\packages3d on Windows). These models are:

  • ESP-12E by Jerry Dunmire (jdunmire) (under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License)
  • Molex 530470810, 530480810, 1050170001 by Molex
  • MicroswitchSmd_4.3 by Würth Elektronik
  • Other are custom made
